# Journey A -- Nutrimix Repeat Purchase: Insights

## Executive Summary

LittleJoys Nutrimix achieved a 46.0% first-purchase rate after a 20-day stimulus sequence. However, only 82.6% of first-time buyers reordered at day 60 -- a drop of -36.6 percentage points. The primary driver of lapse was 'no discount available'. Reorderers were driven primarily by 'within budget comfort zone'. The brand trust trajectory shows a 0.00-point change between day 20 (trust: 0.00) and day 32 (trust: 0.00), coinciding with competitor retargeting. Recommendation: introduce a post-purchase reinforcement stimulus between days 25-35 to defend trust before the reorder window opens.
